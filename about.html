Behind the Automation

About Me

I'm Karel Durovic, also known online as LorDury — an IT technician, automation enthusiast, AI coach, and occasional 3D printing tinkerer.

"Most of my interests revolve around systems — whether it’s a game, a workflow, a city, or a machine. I like understanding how things fit together and how small changes can improve the whole."

Personal Story

I live in Jinočany and work in Holešovice, Prague, currently at Accenture as an IT technician. My path has always been about making things work a bit smoother — whether it’s computers, workflows, or everyday routines.

Outside of work, life is happily busy: my spouse is on maternity leave, and we have two daughters — one starting school and one newborn. So yes, there’s a lot of noise, a lot of love, and not a lot of sleep.

Work & Focus

Professionally, I focus on troubleshooting, support, and optimizing workflows so that people spend less time fighting their tools. Over time, I’ve shifted more towards automation and scripting:

  • BAT scripting and PowerShell for everyday IT tasks
  • Python for automation and small utilities (including PyAutoGUI)
  • Power Automate and Power Apps for low‑cost solutions

My philosophy is simple: automation should be accessible without needing enterprise budgets.

Professional Journey

I’ve moved from core IT operations into automation and learning support. A few of the roles that shaped this path include:

  • Training & Performance Support Developer — building hands-on AI and Power Platform training.
  • Application Automation Engineer — improving onboarding and asset workflows for support teams.
  • Technology OpS Support Practitioner — managing workstation fleets and resolving technical issues.

The common thread is this: help people work better with technology by removing repetitive work and making tools easier to use.
