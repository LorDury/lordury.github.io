Onboarding Automation

This automation addressed a recurring bottleneck in onboarding: manual password resets and handover steps slowed deployments and created unnecessary friction.

  • Built a Python and Selenium workflow for password generation, resets, and verification.
  • Removed repetitive manual tasks from the onboarding process.
  • Created a faster, more consistent experience for new-user setup.

Resale Laptops Application

The goal was to improve the communication and tracking around laptop refreshes and resale handovers, where confusion and back-and-forth were common.

  • Built a lightweight asset-management tool for tracking device transitions.
  • Made handovers clearer for support teams and stakeholders.
  • Reduced unnecessary communication noise during the resale flow.

Compliance & Support Tooling

This work focused on turning manual compliance checks into reliable automated routines so support teams could spend less time reacting and more time preventing issues.

  • Supported a more proactive approach to support and maintenance.
