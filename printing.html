3D Printing

I use 3D printing to solve practical problems, create useful accessories, and turn ideas into physical objects quickly. I care a lot about function and safety, but I also enjoy decorative work, small artistic details, and making things that feel personal.

How I Work

I start with a need, a reference, or a visual idea, then design a simple version and test it. If it works, I refine it so it is durable, useful, and pleasant to look at. The same approach applies whether I’m creating a practical replacement part, a decorative object, or a faithful model of something real.
